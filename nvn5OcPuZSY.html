Post-work life: What to do with your retirement savings

When you retire, you may have a lot of questions about what to do with your retirement savings. Here are some tips to help you make the most of your retirement funds: 1. Make a plan. Start planning for retirement as soon as you start working, and create a roadmap for saving. Determine how much you need to save each month to have enough money saved by the time you retire, and track your progress. 2. Invest your money. Open a retirement account with a reputable company, and invest your money in safe, stable investments such as bonds or mutual funds. 3. Take advantage of tax breaks. Many 401(k) and IRA accounts offer tax breaks such as pre-tax contributions and tax-deferred growth. Use these breaks to your advantage to save more money. 4. Use your benefits. Many companies offer retirement benefits such as pensions and 401(k) plans. Check with your employer to see if you are eligible for benefits and what benefits are available. 5. Consider an annuity.
