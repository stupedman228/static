Disability benefits can help boost your income

When considering Disability benefits, it’s important to understand the many different types of benefits that are available. Here are a few key points to keep in mind: • Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) can provideincome replacement for as long as you are unable to work due to a physical or mental impairment. • Supplemental Social Security Income (SSI) is a government-funded payment that can provide a nutritional, housing, and other basic needs. • Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) is a government program that helps families who need assistance but don’t meet the financial requirements for SSI or SSDI. • Income-protection benefits can provide coverage while you are not able to work because of a serious injury or illness. It is important to know that eligibility for all of these benefits depends on your specific situation. If you are considering applying for disability benefits, it is important to speak to an experienced benefits attorney to figure out your eligibility and payouts.
